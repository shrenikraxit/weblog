The game begins in silence — just eight cards and a chance to outthink your opponent. Every choice carries risk, and the first mistake often decides the battle. Let's Clash

What Is Clash Royale?

Clash Royale is a real-time strategy game created by Supercell where two players battle on a small arena with three towers each. Every player brings a deck of eight cards made up of troops, spells, and buildings, and each card costs a certain amount of elixir to play.

The main goal of the game is to destroy more towers than your opponent before the timer runs out. Players place cards on the battlefield, and those troops automatically walk toward the enemy side, attacking anything in their way. Because elixir slowly refills over time, good players must decide when to defend, when to attack, and how to get the most value from every card.

Best Evos

Evo Mega Knight is one of the best evolutions in Clash Royale because his upgraded spawn and jump damage make him even more devastating at controlling pushes. He can wipe out swarms and severely punish overcommitting opponents with ease, turning defense into a powerful counterattack.

Evo Archers stand out thanks to their incredible burst damage and quick-fire capability. They shred both swarms and high-HP units, making them versatile on offense and defense. Their pressure makes them a top-tier evolution in many competitive decks.

Evo Knight gains boosted durability and stronger attacks, making him extremely reliable in almost any matchup. For only 3 elixir, he controls space, soaks damage, and supports counterpushes with consistent value.

Evo Goblin Barrel provides massive burst damage and forces precise responses from opponents. The evolved Goblins hit harder, survive longer, and punish even small defensive mistakes, making it a dominant win condition.

Best Cards

The Witch constantly spawns Skeletons to tank shots and distract troops, making her strong on defense. When placed behind a tank, she can build a powerful counterpush.

Wall Breakers are a high-risk, high-reward pressure card. If the enemy mistimes their defense, they explode for massive tower damage — all for just 2 elixir.

Miner can be placed anywhere in the arena, allowing players to chip towers or target backline troops. He’s flexible and fits into many control or cycle decks.

The Log knocks back enemy troops, clears swarms, and chips towers. It’s one of the most efficient spells in the entire game for just 2 elixir.
